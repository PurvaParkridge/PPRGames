Tournament Overview

Welcome to the official guide for our multi-sport tournament in PPR. This event features Tennis, Cricket, Football, Badminton, and TT. Each sport is structured as a high-stakes, single-elimination knockout tournament.

While individual sports are knockout-based, the overall tournament champion is determined by a cumulative points system. Teams earn points for wins in the various events. Please see Format for more details


Use the navigation to explore the tournament format, team rules, the full schedule, scoring system, and to track live standings on the leaderboard.

Knockout Format Explained

Overall event is team based. All teams need to follow the criteria mentioned below:

  • 1. All blocks (A, B, C, D and E) need to come up with their teams
  • 2. All teams should come up with a "team name" and should select their captain
  • 3. All team members need to be from the same block
  • 4. We want good participation from all. Hence, each team needs to have at least 10 members which should include at least 2 ladies and 2 kids < 16 years
  • 5. All teams need to play all events. Hence, teams should be formed in a way so that all sports could be covered


There is a special individual walking event. This will be under following 2 age groups:
- between 45 and 55
- greater than 55
It will not be part of the team scoring, but a separate prize will be awarded to the individual who covers the longest distance during the duration of the tournament.

Scoring System

Overall event is team based. A points system tracks team performance. Even in a knockout format, every match counts towards the overall championship. The team with the highest cumulative score across all the games wins the tournament. Hence, it is important to participate in all the games. Farther you go, more points you get
